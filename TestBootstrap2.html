Switzerland

Switzerland, offically Swiss Confederation. Country, central Europe. Area: 15,943 sq mi (41,291 sq km), Population: (2023 est.) 8.864.000. Capitals: Bern (administrative). Lausanne Gudicial




Population

The population is German, French, and Italian, Languages: German, French, Italian Romansh (all offical. with Romansh used locally). Religions: Christianity (Roman Catholic Protestant): also Islam. Currency: Swiss franc. Switzerland is divided into three regions: the meadow-covered Jura Mountains: the central Mittelland, a rich agricultural and urbanized area; and the lofty crags of the Alps.




Economy

It is one of the world's major financial centres: its economy is based largely on international trade and banking, as well as light and heavy industries. Manufactures indude watches, precision instruments, machinery, and chemicals. Tourism and agriculture are also important; products include grains, sugar beets, fruits and vegetables, dairy products, chocolate and wine
